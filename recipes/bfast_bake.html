Blueberry Cottage Cheese Breakfast Bake

Description

This is a breakfast bake made with cottage cheese and blueberries.
Perfect for cozy weekend mornings!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Set your oven to 350F (175C) and grease a small baking dish.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine cottage cheese, rolled oats, eggs, honey (or maple syrup), cinnamon, and vanilla extract. Stir until everything is well combined.
  3. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries to ensure they're evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  4. Pour the mixture into the greased baking dish and spread it evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Allow the bake to cool slightly before slicing. Enjoy it warm, or store leftovers in the refrigerator for a quick grab-and-go breakfast throughout the week.
